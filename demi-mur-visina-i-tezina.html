What is a Demi Mur?

A demi mur, often referred to as a half wall, is a structural element that does not extend from floor to ceiling. Typically, demi murs can be found in residential homes, commercial spaces, and public buildings. They can be made from a variety of materials including bricks, concrete, and drywall, depending on the intended use and aesthetic preference.
