What is Jeff Bezos' Biography?

Jeff Bezos was born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico. He exhibited an early aptitude for science and technology, which later served as the foundation for his groundbreaking ventures. After graduating from Princeton University with degrees in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Bezos embarked on a career on Wall Street before launching Amazon in 1994. The company began as an online bookstore and has since evolved into a global e-commerce giant, revolutionizing the way consumers shop.
