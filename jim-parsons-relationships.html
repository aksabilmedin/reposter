What is Jim Parsons’ Biography?

Jim Parsons was born on March 24, 1973, in Houston, Texas. He pursued a Bachelor of Arts degree in Theatre at the University of Houston and later earned a Master of Fine Arts degree from the University of San Diego. His career took off with the role of Sheldon Cooper, which earned him multiple Emmy Awards and a dedicated fan base. Apart from acting, Parsons has also ventured into producing and has appeared in various films and stage productions.
