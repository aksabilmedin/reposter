Who is Morgan Freeman? A Brief Biography

Morgan Freeman was born on June 1, 1937, in Memphis, Tennessee. He grew up in a modest household and developed a passion for acting at a young age. After honing his craft in various theater productions, he made his film debut in 1964 with "The Pawnbroker." However, it was not until the 1990s that he gained widespread recognition for his performances in films like "Driving Miss Daisy," "The Shawshank Redemption," and "Million Dollar Baby," earning him numerous awards and accolades, including an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor.
