What is Tania Raymonde's Biography?

Tania Raymonde was born on March 22, 1988, in Belleville, Paris, France. She moved to the United States at a young age and began her acting career early on. Tania gained widespread recognition for her role as Alex Rousseau in the critically acclaimed television series "Lost," which aired from 2004 to 2010. Her performance on the show was pivotal in establishing her as a talented actress capable of handling complex roles.
