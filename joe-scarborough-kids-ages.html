Joe Scarborough is an American television host, attorney, and former politician. Scarborough attended the University of Alabama, where he earned a degree in history, and went on to receive his Juris Doctor from the University of Florida College of Law. He began his political career as a Republican congressman, serving Florida's 1st congressional district from 1995 to 2001.

Following his time in Congress, Scarborough transitioned to broadcasting, where he gained fame for his engaging style and insightful commentary on political matters. He co-founded "Morning Joe" in 2007, which quickly became a staple in morning news programming. His ability to dissect complex political issues while maintaining a conversational tone has garnered him a loyal audience.
