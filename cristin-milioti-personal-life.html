What is Cristin Milioti's Biography?

Cristin Milioti was born on August 16, 1985, in Cherry Hill, New Jersey. She graduated from Cherry Hill High School East and later attended the New York University Tisch School of the Arts, where she honed her craft in theater. Milioti first gained recognition for her work in the Broadway musical "Once," which earned her a Tony Award nomination. Her career has since expanded to include notable roles in television series such as "How I Met Your Mother," in which she played the iconic role of Tracy McConnell, and in films like "The Wolf of Wall Street."
