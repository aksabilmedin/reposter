What is Caroline Kennedy's Biography?

Caroline Bouvier Kennedy was born on November 27, 1957, in New York City. As the daughter of the 35th President of the United States, her upbringing was steeped in both privilege and tragedy. She attended Harvard University, where she earned her bachelor's degree in 1980. Later, she obtained her law degree from Columbia Law School. Throughout her career, Caroline has served in various capacities, including as the U.S. Ambassador to Japan from 2013 to 2017.
