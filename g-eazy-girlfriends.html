Who is G-Eazy? A Brief Biography

Born Gerald Earl Gillum on May 24, 1989, in Oakland, California, G-Eazy is an American rapper, singer, and record producer. He rose to fame with his hit singles and albums that showcase his distinctive style, blending elements of hip-hop, rock, and pop. G-Eazy attended Loyola University New Orleans, where he studied music industry studies, and began his music career in earnest after releasing his debut album, "Must Be Nice," in 2013. His subsequent albums, including "When It's Dark Out" and "The Beautiful & Damned," solidified his status in the music industry.
