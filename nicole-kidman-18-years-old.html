What is Nicole Kidman's Biography?

Nicole Kidman was born on June 20, 1967, in Honolulu, Hawaii, to Australian parents. She spent the majority of her childhood in Sydney, Australia, where she developed a passion for acting at a young age. Kidman's breakthrough came when she starred in the Australian film "Bush Christmas" at the age of 16, but it was her role in "Days of Thunder" alongside Tom Cruise that truly launched her career.
