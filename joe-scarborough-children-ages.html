Who is Joe Scarborough? A Brief Biography

Joe Scarborough is an American television host, lawyer, and politician who gained fame as the co-host of MSNBC's "Morning Joe." Born on April 9, 1963, in Meridian, Mississippi, Scarborough graduated from the University of Alabama with a degree in history and later earned his Juris Doctor from the University of Florida. He served as a U.S. Congressman from Florida's 1st congressional district from 1995 to 2001 before transitioning to a career in media.
