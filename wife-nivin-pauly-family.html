Who is Nivin Pauly?

Nivin Pauly, born on October 11, 1984, in Alappuzha, Kerala, is a celebrated actor and producer known for his contributions to Malayalam cinema. He made his acting debut in 2010 with the film 'Malarvaadi Arts Club' and quickly rose to fame with hits like 'Premam,' 'Neram,' and 'Kayamkulam Kochunni.'
