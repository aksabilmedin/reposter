What is the Biography of Kelly Piquet?

Kelly Piquet was born on December 7, 1988, in Homburg, Germany. She is the daughter of Brazilian racing legend Nelson Piquet and his former partner, Cidinha Piquet. Growing up in a family with deep roots in motorsport, Kelly developed a keen interest in the racing world. Her father’s influence and legacy played a pivotal role in shaping her career choices.
