What is Lauren Graham's Biography?

Lauren Graham was born on March 16, 1967, in Honolulu, Hawaii. She grew up in a diverse family, with her father working as a candy executive and her mother as a former professional dancer. Graham's early love for storytelling and performing led her to pursue acting, eventually earning her a spot in the prestigious Barnard College of Columbia University. After graduating, she moved to New York City to pursue her dreams in theater and television.
