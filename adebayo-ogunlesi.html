What is Adebayo Ogunlesi's Biography?

Adebayo Ogunlesi was born on March 2, 1953, in Nigeria. He was raised in a family that valued education, which laid the foundation for his future successes. Ogunlesi attended the prestigious University of Victoria in Canada, where he earned a Bachelor of Arts in Economics. He later pursued his education at Harvard Law School, graduating with a Juris Doctor degree. His academic achievements and intellectual prowess set the stage for a flourishing career in finance and investment.

How Did Adebayo Ogunlesi Become a Leader in Investment?

Adebayo Ogunlesi's journey in the investment world began after he completed his education. He started his career at the investment bank Credit Suisse First Boston, where he honed his skills in finance and investment banking. His remarkable talent and dedication soon led him to the ranks of senior management, where he played a crucial role in executing significant transactions and investments.

In 2006, Ogunlesi founded Global Infrastructure Partners, a highly successful investment firm focused on infrastructure projects worldwide. Under his leadership, GIP has raised billions of dollars and invested in key sectors such as energy, transportation, and water. Ogunlesi's strategic vision and ability to identify lucrative opportunities have positioned GIP as a leader in the infrastructure investment space.
