What is Nivin Pauly's Biography?

Nivin Pauly is an acclaimed Indian actor and producer, primarily known for his work in the Malayalam film industry. Born on October 11, 1984, in Aluva, Kerala, Nivin has made a significant impact on Indian cinema with his charming performances and versatility. He completed his engineering degree from Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT) before venturing into the film industry. His career began with a supporting role in the film "Malarvaadi Arts Club," but he gained fame with movies like "Premam," "Bangalore Days," and "Action Hero Biju."
