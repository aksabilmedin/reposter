What is Jeff Bezos's Biography?

Jeff Bezos was born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico. He displayed an early interest in technology and entrepreneurship, which would later define his career. He graduated from Princeton University in 1986 with degrees in electrical engineering and computer science. After working on Wall Street in various capacities, Bezos founded Amazon in 1994, initially as an online bookstore. Over the years, Amazon expanded into a global e-commerce giant, revolutionizing the way people shop.
