What is Kelly Piquet's Biography?

Kelly Piquet was born on December 7, 1988, in Homburg, Germany. She is the daughter of three-time Formula One World Champion Nelson Piquet and his former partner, Cidinha Piquet. Growing up in a household where racing was a way of life, she developed a deep passion for motorsports from a young age. Kelly pursued her education in International Relations at the University of São Paulo, Brazil, which helped her navigate the complexities of the sports industry.
