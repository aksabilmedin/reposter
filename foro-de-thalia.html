Who is Thalía? A Brief Biography

Born Ariadna Thalía Sodi Miranda on August 26, 1971, in Mexico City, Thalía is a renowned singer, actress, businesswoman, and author. With a career spanning over three decades, she has made a lasting impact on the Latin music scene and has become a household name in numerous countries. Her rise to fame began with her participation in the girl group Din Din, but it was her role in popular telenovelas such as "Marimar," "Maria la del Barrio," and "Rosalinda" that solidified her status as a cultural icon.
