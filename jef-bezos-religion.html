What is Jeff Bezos' Biography?

Jeff Bezos was born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico. He showed an early interest in technology and entrepreneurship, leading him to pursue a degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Princeton University. After graduation, Bezos worked on Wall Street, where he developed a keen eye for innovative business opportunities. In 1994, he took a leap of faith and founded Amazon.com, which began as an online bookstore and quickly evolved into the e-commerce giant we know today.
